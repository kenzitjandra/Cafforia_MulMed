Coffee

The

Cappuccino

A delicate yet indulgent brew, the Cappuccino harmonizes bold espresso with velvety steamed milk and a crown of airy foam. Its origins trace back to 17th-century Europe, inspired by the Capuchin monks' distinctive robes. Each cup delivers a rich, smooth texture—an elegant classic for those who savor coffee with a touch of luxury.

Cafforia empowers coffee enthusiasts by offering expert-guided tutorials on brewing techniques, from classic espresso to specialty pour-overs.
